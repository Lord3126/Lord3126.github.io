What Makes Jaws The Most Iconic?

Jaws has to be the worlds most iconic movie. This is thanks to its influence on the film industry — being the first ‘event’ movie, and shown you can still make money off of a big budget. Jaws took over the movie marketing and merchandising industry with its new film. Amazing graphics for its time, and simply one of the biggest releases ever.
