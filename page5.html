How Has Film Evolved?

July 31, 1891, does that speak any significance? Well, that is the date the first black and white motion pictures camera was made (no sound). From 1902 - 1906, colored films, narrative films, Feature films, and animated films had all made their debuts. In 1927 and 1928, the first film as well as animated film has dialogue! 3-D was first introduced in 1953. And then in November of 1995, the first full featured computer generated imagery film was released, that happened to be Toy Story! In 2009, James Cameron was the first person to have a Full Performance Capture Film, very unique!
